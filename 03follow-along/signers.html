Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence.
John Adams
Future 2nd U.S. President and key advocate for independence.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and elder statesman.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, known for his bold signature.
Samuel Adams
Cousin of John Adams and a strong leader in the push for independence.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their courage in signing the Declaration, an act considered treasonous by the British Crown.
