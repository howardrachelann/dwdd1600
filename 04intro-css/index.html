About

Meet the Artist

Hi! My name's Rachel, the hands (and heart) behind the nails. I started doing nails as a fun creative outlet and quickly fell in love with the process—from shaping and prepping to the final top coat. What began as doing nails for friends and family turned into a true passion for helping others feel confident and put-together. I'm all about fun sets, healthy nails, and a relaxing experience. Whether you're coming in for a bold new look or a simple refresh, I want you to leave feeling like your best self.

The Nail Standard

At the core of everything I do is a commitment to quality, health, and creativity. I believe that beautiful nails start with a healthy foundation, which is why I prioritize natural nail care, clean techniques, and high-quality products. Every set is done with intention—whether it's a minimalist look or a bold design, I aim for precision, durability, and style that fits you. My goal is to create a space where you can relax, feel taken care of, and leave with nails you're proud to show off. No shortcuts, no harsh drilling—just thoughtful work and good vibes.

What to Expect

  1. A welcoming, clean, and cozy space.
  2. One-on-one attention and no rushed appointments.
  3. Gentle prep that protects your natural nails.
  4. High-quality, non-toxic gels and products.
  5. A relaxing vibe with good convo (or quiet if you prefer!).
  6. Customized designs to match your style.
  7. Clear aftercare tips to keep your nails lasting longer.
  8. No overbooking, no double clients—your time is yours.
